Business Intentions

Discount Review

A mid-sized online retailer operating across the UK wanted to better understand sales performance across product categories and evaluate the impact of discounts on revenue generation. As part of their 2024 strategic review, the business needed clear visibility into category-level trends, customer engagement, and promotional efficiency.

Dashboard Goals

  • Track year-to-date revenue, customer growth, and daily performance
  • Compare category sales trends across months
  • Analyse discount effectiveness by category
  • Identify record performance days and highlight key drivers
  • Spot underperforming segments despite promotional efforts

Data Handling Process

  • Data Import: Imported pandas library and the e-commerce CSV dataset, checking data types for potential issues
  • Date Conversion: Resolved Purchase_Date column being read as object instead of datetime using parse_dates and pd.to_datetime() with proper format specification
  • Quality Assurance: Implemented error handling with 'coerce' parameter to manage invalid datetime values
  • Final Validation: Performed comprehensive checks for null values and duplicate rows to ensure data integrity

Key Insights

Electronics Strategy Rethink

Despite being the top-selling category, Electronics shows weak sales performance relative to discount levels applied. Consider optimising discount strategy or focusing on higher-margin products within this category.

Reflections & Learnings

The initial experience of creating this dashboard was an attempt at experimenting with a random dataset and seeing what visuals I could produce. I ended up with a mess of a dashboard, filled with meaningless or boring visuals that told no story.

After working with Ben's Pizza, I had a much clearer idea of what the purpose of a dashboard is. It was no longer about the sheer mass of information, but more about presenting data in a way that's actually useful.

This refurbishment project taught me the importance of business context, visual hierarchy, and storytelling with data. The result is a dashboard that not only looks professional but serves as a genuine decision-making tool.
